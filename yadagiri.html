Yadagirigutta, now known as Yadadri, is a revered pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Lakshmi Narasimha, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. Located near Hyderabad in Telangana, the temple is built on a hillock and has recently undergone a majestic architectural transformation resembling grand South Indian temple designs.

The deity is worshipped in multiple forms – Jwala Narasimha, Yogananda Narasimha, and Lakshmi Narasimha – making it unique among Narasimha temples. The atmosphere is peaceful, and it attracts thousands of devotees daily. Yadadri is considered Telangana’s Tirupati, due to its rising popularity and massive reconstruction by the state government.
