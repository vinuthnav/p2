Papikondalu, also known as Papi Hills, is a scenic and spiritual destination located along the Godavari River in Andhra Pradesh. Though not a temple town itself, the hills are closely associated with religious boat trips and nature-based pilgrimages, especially en route to Bhadrachalam. The name "Papikondalu" comes from the mountain range's resemblance to a woman's hair parting, symbolizing divine femininity.

Devotees often take boat journeys from Rajahmundry to Bhadrachalam, passing through these majestic hills. The river cruise offers serene views of nature and dense forests while carrying spiritual significance due to the proximity to Rama’s exile route mentioned in legends. Papikondalu thus blends natural beauty with sacred folklore.
