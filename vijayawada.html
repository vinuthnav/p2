Vijayawada, a major city in Andhra Pradesh, is famous for the Kanaka Durga Temple, situated on the Indrakeeladri Hill overlooking the Krishna River. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Kanaka Durga, believed to be a powerful incarnation of Shakti who slayed the demon Mahishasura at this site.

Navaratri and Dasara festivals are celebrated here with grandeur, attracting lakhs of pilgrims from across the country. The temple offers breathtaking views of the city and river, while its rich legends and historical relevance make it a spiritual anchor for the region. It is also easily accessible from across Andhra Pradesh.
